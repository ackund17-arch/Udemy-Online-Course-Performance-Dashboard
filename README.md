# Udemy-Online-Course-Performance-Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Udemy's business performance through revenue, subscriber growth, course categories, payment types, and yearly trends. The dashboard provides actionable insights to support strategic decisions in online education and business growth.

Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides an interactive analysis of Udemy's online course performance by monitoring key business metrics such as revenue, costs, subscribers, courses, reviews, and lecturers. It enables stakeholders to evaluate platform growth, identify top-performing course categories, understand subscriber behavior, and monitor revenue trends over time through interactive visualizations.

The dashboard transforms raw educational business data into meaningful insights that support strategic decision-making and business growth.

Business Problem

Online learning platforms generate vast amounts of operational data from course enrollments, payments, reviews, and instructors. Without a centralized reporting solution, it becomes difficult for management to identify high-performing course categories, monitor revenue growth, evaluate customer engagement, and make informed business decisions.

This dashboard addresses these challenges by presenting key performance indicators in a single interactive view.

Project Objectives
Monitor Udemy's overall business performance.
Analyze revenue generated across different course categories.
Compare subscriber distribution between paid and free users.
Evaluate revenue contribution by course level.
Track yearly revenue trends.
Enable interactive exploration using filters for course level, payment type, and quarter.

Key Insights

The platform generated approximately $882 million in total revenue while maintaining relatively low operational costs.
Web Development is the highest revenue-generating course category, significantly outperforming other categories.
Nearly 70% of subscribers are paid users, indicating a strong monetization strategy.
Courses classified under All Levels contribute the highest share of total revenue.
Revenue experienced rapid growth between 2014 and 2015 before gradually declining in subsequent years.
Web Development also attracts the largest subscriber base, highlighting sustained learner demand for technology-related courses.


Business Value

This dashboard enables educational platform managers and business executives to monitor financial performance, understand learner behavior, identify profitable course categories, and optimize content strategy. By consolidating multiple performance indicators into a single interactive dashboard, it supports faster, data-driven decision-making and strategic planning.
